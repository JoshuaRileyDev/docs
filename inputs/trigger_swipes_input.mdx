---
title: "Trigger Swipes Input"
---

Swipes input allows you to covertly swipe with your finger on your phone as an input.

The way it works is for every swipe you do it’ll be either **1-4**, for example if you swipe **up** then it’ll be **1**, if you swipe **down** then it’ll be **3**.

You can swipe as many times as you want to join the numbers together, so for example if you swiped **up** and **left** then it would output **14**.

By default there is a **3 second** timer that gets reset for every swipe you do, after your last swipe it’ll then count up to 3 to lock your input in and output it via the **Return Input** shortcut.

This means you have a **maximum** of **3 seconds** to make each swipe for it to count towards the final output.
